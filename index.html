Shoebox

A smarter, paperless future for receipts.

Shoebox transforms everyday receipts into valuable data — empowering both consumers and merchants.

For merchants: Get deep insights into customer purchasing trends, track basket sizes, and understand peak transaction times. Use this data to optimize marketing campaigns, drive loyalty programs, and reduce waste from paper receipts.

For consumers: Store your receipts securely in one place, track spending, and access useful summaries for budgeting and returns. Never lose a proof of purchase again.

Shoebox integrates with existing POS systems and parses receipt data via email — no hardware required.

Join us as we build the modern infrastructure for receipt data, starting with a digital-first experience for independent retailers.
